Hyderabad

Tankbund

Tank Bund is a picturesque promenade along the Hussain Sagar lake in Hyderabad, India. It's a popular destination for locals and tourists alike, offering stunning views of the lake and the city skyline. Visitors can enjoy leisurely walks, bike rides, or simply relax on the benches while taking in the serene atmosphere. The Tank Bund is also home to several iconic landmarks, including the Buddha statue and the Necklace Road. It's a must-visit place for anyone exploring Hyderabad

Transportation

  • By Auto :

    This is the most common and convenient way. Just flag down an auto and tell the driver "Tank Bund"

  • By Bus: Several buses pass through Tank Bund. Check the bus schedule or ask a local for the correct bus number

  • By Metro: The Hyderabad Metro has a Khairthabad station near Tank Bund. From there, it's a short walk to the promenade

  • By Taxi: Book a taxi through a mobile app or hail one on the street
